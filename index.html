Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels are social animals who roam the deserts in search of food and water.
  • Camels have two rows of thick eyelashes to protect their eyes from the desert dust
  • A thirsty camel can drink up to 135 liters in one sitting.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • Chameleons have amazing eyes. The bulging upper and lower eyelids are joined and the pupil peaks out from a pinhole sized gap.
  • The chameleons eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs.This gives them a full 360-degree field of vision.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • They can find their way back to their nests from 1300 miles away.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bear

  • The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
  • In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • They have great camouflage for their environment
  • Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours
  • Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm-size of a pencil
